Life After Doping

Rebuilding a Future

Career Options

  • Sports Coaching: Guide and train athletes while promoting clean sports.
  • Fitness Trainer: Help individuals achieve their fitness goals naturally.
  • Anti-Doping Advocacy: Educate young athletes about ethical sports practices in NADA or WADA.
  • NADA or WADA: Explore career options or volunteer opportunities. Visit NADA.
  • Sports Authority of India (SAI): Apply for support staff or other job roles. Visit SAI Jobs.
  • Police Force: Roles as constables, sub-inspectors, or trainers in police academies.

Support and Rehabilitation

  • Rehabilitation Programs: Join counseling or rehabilitation initiatives.
  • Community Service: Engage in programs to give back to society and rebuild trust.
  • Mentorship: Share personal experiences to inspire and educate others.
  • Personal Trainer: Help others achieve fitness goals in gyms or private settings.
  • Sports Rehabilitation Specialist: Assist athletes recovering from injuries or doping impacts.

Educational Opportunities

  • Professional Certifications: Obtain credentials in fitness, sports science, or coaching.
  • Higher Education: Pursue advanced degrees in sports management or health sciences.
  • Workshops and Seminars: Attend events focused on ethical practices in sports.

Entrepreneurship Opportunities

  • Start a Sports Academy: Focus on clean training for athletes.
  • Start an EdTech Company: Provide certifications and education about anti-doping.
