Rules and Regulations

2024

Do's

  • Educate yourself on anti-doping rules and regulations.
  • Consult a healthcare professional before using any medications.
  • Report any suspicious activities related to doping.
  • Maintain integrity and fairness in sports.
  • Check the prohibited list on the WADA or NADA website regularly.

Don'ts

  • Do not use performance-enhancing drugs or supplements without verification.
  • Do not ignore the rules set by WADA and NADA.
  • Do not share or use substances from unverified sources.
  • Do not avoid drug testing or attempt to tamper with results.
  • Do not promote or encourage the use of banned substances.
